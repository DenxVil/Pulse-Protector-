Deployment Protocol

2-Minute Response Workflow

Step-by-step protocol for rapid trauma response

1
T = 0-15s

Scene Safety Assessment

Ensure the scene is safe for both responder and patient. Identify hazards.

2
T = 15-30s

Check for Bleeding

Rapid assessment of visible hemorrhage and life-threatening bleeding.

3
T = 30-60s

Apply Tourniquet

If extremity hemorrhage detected, apply CAT tourniquet. Note time of application.

4
T = 60-90s

Check Breathing

Assess airway patency and respiratory effort. Use Ambu bag if needed.

5
T = 90-120s

Attach Smart Pad

Place Smart Vital Monitoring Pad on patient for continuous monitoring.

6
T = 2+ min

Data Transmission

Vital signs automatically transmitted to receiving hospital for triage preparation.
