Verification

How to Verify These References

Auditable steps for judges to confirm source authenticity

1. PubMed (PMID)

Go to pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov, enter the PMID (e.g., 19106674) in the search box. You'll see the abstract, authors, journal, and citation count.

2. DOI Search

Go to doi.org, type the DOI (e.g., 10.1001/jama.2010.1140). You'll be redirected to the publisher's official page with full citation details.

3. Google Scholar

Search scholar.google.com using author name + year (e.g., "Kragh 2009 tourniquet"). Check citation count and where the paper is cited.

4. Official Publishers

For guidelines and reports, go directly to who.int, facs.org, or espressif.com to download official PDFs.
