AI Triage Engine

Intelligent decision support for rapid patient prioritization in mass casualty incidents

The AI Triage Engine is a machine learning-powered decision support system that automatically categorizes trauma patients based on vital signs data from the Smart Pad. Using a weighted KPI (Key Performance Indicator) model trained on the START (Simple Triage and Rapid Treatment) protocol, it provides consistent, evidence-based triage recommendations in seconds.
