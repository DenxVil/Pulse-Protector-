2. PPG Sensor Placement (MAX30102)

üí° Primary: Finger Placement

  • Location: Index or middle finger tip
  • Position: Pad side down (fingerprint side)
  • Pressure: Light, non-constrictive strap
  • Advantage: Cleanest signal, easiest access

üí° Alternative: Earlobe Placement

  • Position: Clip sensor gently on earlobe
  • Advantage: Less motion artifact
  • Limitation: Can be uncomfortable
